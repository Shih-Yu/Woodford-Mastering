About...

Pete Johnson has been in the music business since he was a teenager. He has experience as a recording engineer, acoustic specialists as well as a mastering engineer for over 10+ years. Pete resides in Austin, Tx where he does mastering work and is a music educator and MBPT department specialist for the community college
